TASK HUB

Keep Track of Your Tasks

With Your Task Hub, you can easily manage your daily tasks and stay on top of your busy schedule. Whether you're at work, at home, or on-the-go, you can access your task list anytime, anywhere.

Features

Add Tasks

Easily add new tasks to your list by clicking on the "Add Task" button. You can also set due dates, priority levels, and categories to help you stay organized.

Features

Mark as Completed

When you've completed a task, simply check it off your list by clicking on the checkbox. You can also view your completed tasks and keep track of your progress.

Features

Organize Your List

Sort your tasks by due date, priority, or category.

About Us

At Your Task Hub, I believe that everyone can benefit from having a simple and effective tool to manage their daily tasks.
I was inspired to create this project based on my own struggles with staying organized and on top of my task lists.

As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, I decided to create a task list as a way to showcase my skills
and to help others overcome the challenges of task management.
I hope that this project will make a positive impact in people's lives and help them achieve their goals.
